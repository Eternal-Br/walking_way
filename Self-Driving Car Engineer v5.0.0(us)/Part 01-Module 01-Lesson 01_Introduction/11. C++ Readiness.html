11. C++ Readiness

C++ Course to Support Part 1 Sensor Fusion and Part 2 Content

While the initial portion of part 1 does not require C++ knowledge, some part 1 sensor fusion content and a large portion of part 2 coding exercises and projects are primarily C++ based. To prepare you for this, Udacity offers a free C++ course. This course builds on existing programming knowledge and is specifically designed to support self-driving car students. The course can be found here .

C++ Assessment
