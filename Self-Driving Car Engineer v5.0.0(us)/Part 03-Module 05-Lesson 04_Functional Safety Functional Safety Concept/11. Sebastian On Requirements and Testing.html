Quiz: Verification versus Validation

Verification versus validation

What is the difference between verification and validation?

SOLUTION: You verify, for example, that the lane departure warning vibrates the steering wheel at the amount you specified in your safety requirements. You validate that the vibration warning amount is neither too high for a driver to handle nor too low that the driver cannot feel the vibration.
