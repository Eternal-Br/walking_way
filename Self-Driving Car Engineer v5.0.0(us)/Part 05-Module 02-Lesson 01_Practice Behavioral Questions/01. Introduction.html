Introduction

It's time to move on to the behavioral portion of the interview. Behavioral questions are very open-ended and sometimes it's hard to know how to respond; it's hard to know where to start.

In this lesson, you will have the opportunity to answer and reflect on interview questions generated by our own hiring managers, engineers, and students. Through practice you can become comfortable with the types of behavioral questions you are likely to encounter.
